Family Tradition Since Generations

Our olive oil comes from a family business located in the beautiful region of Pelion, Volos. The olive trees are 200-300 years old, standing as living witnesses to the rich agricultural heritage of Greece.

These ancient trees produce olives of exceptional quality. Our production is fully organic, with no pesticides or chemicals used. Each drop of our extra virgin olive oil preserves the authentic taste and health benefits of this Greek treasure.

Due to the limited quantity from these heritage trees, our olive oil is a premium product for those who appreciate quality and tradition.
